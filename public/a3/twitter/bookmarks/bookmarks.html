Pilbara Minerals @PLS - Sep 14
2240 dollars! Pilbara lithium concentrate auction drop hammer, estimated lithium price may exceed 200000 yuan
Source: Haitong Metal formation
Pilbara completed its second lithium concentrate auction on September 14th. At the end of July, against the backdrop of a bid of US $740 for lithium concentrate, Pilbara sold at its first auction of US $1250 and a high price of US $1315 in Hong Kong, breaking the window of a spot shortage of lithium concentrate. Since then, the price of lithium products has accelerated. During the two auctions, the price of lithium concentrate rose from $740 to $990, the price of electric carbon rose from 90, 000 yuan to 144000 yuan, and the price of lithium hydroxide rose from 97500 yuan to 144000 yuan. It can be said that the Pilbara auction price contributes to the lithium price.
